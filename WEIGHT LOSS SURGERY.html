WEIGHT LOSS SURGERY

Lamaa Medical Tourism offers all interventional surgical procedures for those patients who have tried other methods and have not yet achieved the optimum physical results that they desire and deserve to realize. Conventional Weight Loss methodologies can be quite daunting and seem like an uphill task, but with the advancements in medical science and technology we have the option of achieving our physical aspirations quite remarkably without much effort. Below is the list of innovative interventional procedures that Lamaa medical Tourism can facilitate for you with some of the best doctors and hospitals across Bangalore and Kerala in India and those in Thailand, Turkey and China.
1

Sleeve Gastrectomy

Sleeve Gastrectomy or Gastric Sleeve Surgery is a bariatric laproscopic procedure that involves removing a large portion of the stomach in over weight patients as a last alternative in case other conventional methods have failed to deliver any results. This procedure helps in weight loss by significantly restricting the intake of food.

2

Adjustable Gastric Banding

Adjustable Gastric Banding is a minimally invasive bariatric intervention procedure that involves placing an inflatable band around the top of the stomach for constriction in order for the person to feel fuller with lesser food. This process is completely adjustable as per changing needs as well as reversible whenever desired.

3

Gastric Bypass

Gastric bypass is a procedure that alters the way your digestive system operates and the pattern it uses for absorption. In this surgical procedure the size of the stomach is constricted and some parts of the intestine are bypassed so that the food does not travel to such parts and thus reduce the absorption of calories into the blood.

4

Duodenal Switch

Duodenal switch also known as Bilio Pancreatic Diversion with Duodenal Switch BPD-DS, is an intricate surgical procedure for weight loss that involves restricting the intake of food as well as reducing the absorption of calories in the intestine by combining Sleeve Gastrectomy along with an Intestinal Bypass.
