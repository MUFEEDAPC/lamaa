WHO WE ARE

Lamaa Medical Tourism Private Limited is an organisation that is run by experienced professionals in the field of international medical tourism and hospitality. The founders of Lamaa have decades of experience in this domain and have catered to aspiring patients from various regions of the world. They have for years delivered exemplary services to patients from various Arab and North African nations among others and have been duly recognised by the authorities in these countries for their credibility.

Lamaa boosts of a strong liaison with major health care organizations and hospitality partners across prominent medical destinations like India, Thailand, Turkey and China. At Lamaa we strive to create an unforgettable experience for our clients since we understand the significance of great service and support to ensure a hassle free process that facilitates a stress free treatment.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Abid Hussain...With more than 14 years of robust service experience in the healthcare industry, Mr. Abid Hussain has worked across renowned corporate multi-specialty hospitals in India viz Fortis, Apollo & Nova. Through the course of his illustrious career, Abid has demonstrated exemplary capabilities in the field of patient care and facilitation and has also been duly awarded the best employee of Fortis in Patient Care during his tenure in the organisation.
Following his transition into the medical tourism segment, he has already attained numerous acknowledgements and accolades. His services have been recognized by the Health Ministries in Qatar & Sudan, and he has also been awarded the "best medical tourism facilitator" in Sudan.

ABOUT THE CHAIRMAN

Abdul Aziz Al Ruwaili, Chairman... Our Saudi patron is a seasoned healthcare professional with vast experience in the medical tourism segment. He is also known as Safeer-Ul-Hind due to his experience and exposure in the Indian Subcontinent. He has garnered an extensive experience in both Allopathic as well as Ayurvedic systems of healing across Bangalore and Kerala. He is a recognized expert in the international medical tourism sector.

ABOUT THE CEO

Osama Luthfy CEO: Osama Luthfy is born and brought up in Saudi Arabia. He has always been inclined towards serving others and has found his passion in the field of Medical Tourism. He is the ex-COO of Kottakkal Ayurgarden International a prestigious Ayurveda hospital in Kerala. Osama is a huge enthusiast of Ayurveda and awareness towards Naturopathy and has earned due recognition in Kerala Ayurveda segment. He takes pride in offering Ayurveda treatments to foreign nationals with the holistic approach and service. Being Multi lingual, Osama has been an asset to international medical tourism.
